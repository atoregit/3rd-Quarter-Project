Why did I choose this?

Being jailed for a crime that I did not commit is for me, one of the worst things that can happen to me. It makes it even more tragic if it could be easily be solved if they had a little bit more money. This is why this advocacy has a very noble objective, and that they are the ones that truly give hope to the hopeless without discrimination.

They were the beacons of light during the dark of the Marcos dictatorship, and they continue to be today. Moreover, I cannot appreciate enough how courageous they are to keep defending human rights even faced with threats of death and torture. This is why I chose this advocacy.
